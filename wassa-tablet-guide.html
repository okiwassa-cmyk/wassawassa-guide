WASSA WASSA A small inn by the sea and under the stars · Okinawa Yanbaru

Welcome to WASSA WASSA. We are delighted to have you with us.
Our inn is a small, family-run retreat nestled in the pristine nature of northern Okinawa.
We hope your stay brings you peace, wonder, and unforgettable memories.

🌊
Ocean & Nature
Yanbaru wilderness

Surrounded by the pristine emerald ocean and lush Yanbaru forest. Fireflies illuminate the garden from May to November (45 species).

⭐
Starry Skies
Milky Way above

Far from city lights, our rooftop terrace offers breathtaking views of the Milky Way. Enjoy the outdoor bath under a blanket of stars.

👫
Personal Care
Family-run hospitality

Run by a couple with heartfelt hospitality. We may be small, but every detail of your stay is attended to with genuine warmth and care.

Rooms & Facilities
Two room types and shared amenities
🛏
Superior Twin
2 rooms · 1–4 guests

Spacious one-room design with 3.3m ceilings, earth-tone interiors, and a private garden. From May to November, 45 species of fireflies light up the garden — an unforgettable natural experience.

Beds2 beds (futon provided for 3rd+ guests)
BathroomShared clawfoot bathtub + dual-head shower
AmenitiesTowels, toothbrush, shampoo, conditioner, body wash, razor, cotton swabs
Wi-Fi / A/CIncluded
PatioShared outdoor patio adjacent to bathroom
🏡
Deluxe Twin "Yanbaru Routine"
1 room only · 1–2 guests

A fully private standalone cottage with refined interiors. Escape from everyday life and enjoy the freedom of your own villa.

Beds2 beds (private bathroom included)
BathPrivate "Oil Veil Oxygen Bubble Bath" (moisturizing micro-oil bubbles)
KitchenFull kitchen with cookware and tableware
RooftopExclusive 180° panoramic ocean-view rooftop terrace
TVBuilt-in TV (Superior Twin: available on request)
♨️
Shared Open-Air Bath
Private rental hot spring

Enjoy a private open-air bath with breathtaking ocean views. Swim in the sea by day, then soak under a canopy of stars at night. A truly Okinawan luxury experience.

⚠️ Important

Swimwear must be worn at all times in the open-air bath.

🌅
Rooftop Terrace (42 tsubo)
≈ 139m² shared rooftop

A spacious rooftop terrace with sun loungers overlooking the endless ocean. Watch the sunset, stargaze, or simply relax with the sound of waves. Pure Okinawan bliss.
